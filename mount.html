Mount

Position Description

  • Top player is occupying the space between elbow and knee on both sides of the bottom pleyer. Strattling their waist, facing each other.
  • Knees can be kept high and tight or in a grapevine position.
  • Chest to chest pressure to maintain control.

The mount is a very advantageous position for the top player. Top player can seek many different types of submission while maintaining control. Common submissions include Head and Arm Choke, Armbar, Kumora, and Ezekial choke. Bottom player is trying to escape the position through techniques like the flat leg escape, bump sweep, or other methods.
